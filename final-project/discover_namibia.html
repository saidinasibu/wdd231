Belém Cuisine Attractions

Attractions

Considered the gateway to the Amazon, the capital of Pará has many historical attractions and several outdoor tour options. The main tourist attractions in Belém do Pará include historical complexes, cultural centers, parks and even one of the oldest Brazilian markets still in operation. In addition to tourism in Belém do Pará itself, travelers also have the chance to visit unforgettable scenes on the outskirts of the city, such as Ilha do Combu and Forte do Presépio. The journey continues through the gastronomy of Pará, from the now famous açaí to the traditional duck in tucupi, celebrating the unmistakable Amazonian flavors.
