Overview

Purpose

For my project, I want to create a website about Namibia Safari. This site will be for tourists or anyone interested in going on a safari in Namibia. It will have info about the animals they can see, which parks to visit, some local languages, and travel tips. I want the website to help people explore the country better and know what to expect before they go. The site will have three pages: A homepage with general info about Namibia safaris. A page about the animals (like what they are called and where to find them). A page about discover Namibia (talking about national Namibian's parks) I chose this topic because Iâ€™m from Namibia and I think it would be cool to show more about the nature and wildlife here.
